About Project

The goal of our project is to add equity and inclusion to climate finance

Each of our NFTs include digital and virtual rights to 1 dedicated hectare of mature forestry. This includes 5 digital carbon offsets but no physical rights to the land or trees.Our digital carbon offsets represent future carbon sequestration from live trees.Each NFT is an avatar that you can customize for use in games and metaverses. We also provide a satellite navigation app that allows you to zoom in and see the tree cover on your dedicated hectare for transparent provenance. We geolocate sites of interest in our forest, such as waterfalls and caves. This adds physical rarity to your dedicated hectare and to your NFT. We reinvest 15% of the proceeds from this NFT sale into forest management activities and 50% into a social innovation studio that funds sustainable and profitable projects in the local community.

Problem Statement

Traditional carbon offsets are the problem not the solution.

This NFT sale includes up to 20,000 hectares of mature forestry.

Burning fossil fuels emits carbon dioxide into the atmosphere and forests remove carbon dioxide through photosynthesis.

Trees are most effective at carbon sequestration when they are more than 10 years old.

However, low-income communities are excluded from selling traditional carbon offsets for their mature forests. Their carbon sequestration is regarded as business-as-usual not additional.

Our Hypothesis

If we increase the value of mature forests in emerging economies, deforestation will decline and land owners will plant new trees for future income.

Our Solution

We use blockchain innovations to disrupt the carbon offset industry

We use NFTs, satellite imagery, machine learning and artificial intelligence to disrupt the carbon offset industry. We have created a new class of digital carbon offsets that eliminates expensive intermediaries and reduces certification costs to $0. This adds equity and inclusion to climate finance. Also, we track our digital carbon offsets on a blockchain to prevent double counting.
